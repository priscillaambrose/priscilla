Mission Statement

To provide excellent Mathematics tutoring to your child or ward tailored to the grade six Mathematics curriculum for their Examinations using video tutorials, excersises, games, kinestics and many more learning techniques at reasonable prices.

About the Tutor

Born and raised in the beautiful island of Saint Lucia , I have grown a love for Mathematics over my school years. My name is Priscilla Ambrose and I am a second year student of the Sir Arthur Lewis Community College who has a diversity and broad understanding of the subject Mathematics. As a result, I hope to use this knowledge to aid six graders in my community to better understand Mathematics concepts in respect to their own learning styles. I chose this venture as many students often struggle in the field of Mathematics and thus, several of these kids don't have the sufficient resources to seek assitance in Mathematics. As a result, I decided to provide lessons at very low prices for students in my community hoping to help ease such burdens.
